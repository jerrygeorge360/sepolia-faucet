How to Add Tokens to MetaMask

After claiming tokens, add them to your MetaMask wallet:

  1. Open MetaMask and make sure you're on Sepolia Testnet
  2. Scroll down and click "Import tokens"
  3. Click "Custom token"
  4. Copy and paste the contract address for your token:
DAI: 0xAd22b4EC8cdd8A803d0052632566F6334A04F1F3
USDC: 0xcF9884827F587Cd9a0bDce33995B2333eE7e8285
USDT: 0x1861BB06286aAb0fDA903620844b4Aef4894b719
WBTC: 0x4267652AF61B4bE50A39e700ee2a160f42371f54

MetaMask will automatically detect the token symbol and decimals. After adding, your claimed tokens will appear in your wallet!

Select a token and connect your wallet to get started.
